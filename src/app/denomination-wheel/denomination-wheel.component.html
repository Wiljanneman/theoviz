Explore the hierarchical relationships between Christian denominations and their theological foundations.

Core Christian Beliefs

  • Trinity: One God in three persons
  • Nicene Creed: Historic statement of faith
  • Divinity of Christ: Jesus is fully God
  • Virgin Birth: Miraculous conception
  • Resurrection: Jesus rose from the dead

Major Traditions

Protestant

Emphasizes salvation by faith alone and Scripture alone

Catholic

Papal authority, sacramental tradition, apostolic succession

Eastern Orthodox

Theosis (union with God), liturgical worship, icons

Oriental Orthodox

Miaphysite Christology, ancient traditions

How to Read

Center: Core Christian beliefs shared by all traditions

Ring 2: Major Christian traditions (Protestant, Catholic, Orthodox)

Ring 3: Key theological emphases (SOLA FIDE, SOLA SCRIPTURA)

Ring 4: Specific theological concepts and polity structures

Outer Rings: Individual denominations with their distinctive colors
