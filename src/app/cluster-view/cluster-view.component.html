Card Categories Guide

The 4Ps of Knowing (Vervaeke Framework)

Discover (Propositional)

Uncover what the Scripture teaches. This addresses propositional knowing - factual truth, doctrinal content, and theological principles revealed in the text.

Ask: "What truth is God revealing here?"

Apply (Procedural)

Learn how to practice this teaching. This addresses procedural knowing - concrete actions, spiritual disciplines, and habits to develop based on God's Word.

Ask: "How do I live this out practically?"

Reframe (Perspectival)

Shift how you see the world. This addresses perspectival knowing - changing your worldview, seeing reality differently, and reframing circumstances through God's perspective.

Ask: "How does this change the way I see things?"

Become (Participatory)

Experience transformation in Christ. This addresses participatory knowing - identity formation, relationship with God, and who you're becoming through His refining work.

Ask: "How is God transforming me through this?"

Note: These four types of knowing work together. Biblical study isn't complete with just factual knowledge (Discover) - it must lead to practice (Apply), shape our perspective (Reframe), and transform our identity (Become).
